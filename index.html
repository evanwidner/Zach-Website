My Process

From vision to final frame.

01

Discovery & Concept

We talk through your vision, mood, and goals. I study references, scout locations, and map out a shot list that tells your story.

02

The Shoot

On-location or in-studio — I work fast, direct with intention, and stay loose enough to capture the unexpected. Natural light when possible, shaped light when needed.

03

Cull & Select

I review every frame, narrowing down to the strongest selects. Quality over quantity — only the images that hit land in the final set.

04

Edit & Refine

Each image goes through color grading, tonal adjustments, and retouching in Lightroom and Photoshop. I develop a consistent look that's true to the moment.

05

Delivery

High-res files delivered via private online gallery. Print-ready, social-optimized, and backed up. Your images, finished and forever.
